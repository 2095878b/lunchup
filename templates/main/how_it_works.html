How it works

           It's fast and simple to get started with Lunch Up.

1
   Availablity


  • List the days and times you are free to meetup for lunch

  • The more you add the more connections are made

  • Lunch up will then match you with others


2
 Notifications


  • Once our algorithm matches you up with others, you'll get notifications

  • Check out the profiles of your matches and see what you think

  • If you like what you see, arrange to lunch up! It's that easy.


3
   Lunch Up!


  • Wait to see if your chosen match also wants to Lunch Up

  • If both of you agree, we'll let you know it's on

  • Arrange to meet your lunch match. Enjoy!
